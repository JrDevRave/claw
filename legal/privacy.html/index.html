Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 23, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Claw collects only the minimum information necessary to provide the Service:

  • Account information: Your TikTok username and OAuth tokens when you connect your account.
  • Video content: Video files you upload for processing. These are processed locally on your device and are not stored on our servers.
  • Usage data: Basic usage metrics such as number of clips processed and upload status.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Process your video content into clips
  • Upload clips to your connected social media accounts on your behalf
  • Improve the Service

3. Data Storage and Processing

All video processing happens locally on your device. Your raw footage and generated clips are stored on your local machine only. We do not upload, store, or process your video content on any external server.

4. Third-Party Services

When you connect social media accounts, we use their official APIs to post content on your behalf. We share only what is necessary to complete the upload (video file, title, description, hashtags). We do not sell or share your data with any third party for advertising or marketing purposes.

5. OAuth Tokens

API tokens for connected platforms (TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, Twitter/X) are stored locally on your device in encrypted form. They are never transmitted to our servers.

6. Data Retention

Since all processing is local, you control your own data. Deleting the application removes all locally stored data including tokens and processing history.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Disconnect your social media accounts at any time
  • Delete all locally stored data by removing the application
  • Request information about what data we hold (which is none beyond what's on your device)

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the application.

10. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us via GitHub.
